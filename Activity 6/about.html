Our Founder

Daniel Anthony Belangoy, the founder of Langoy Industries, developed a fascination with marine life at a young age while growing up near the Philippine coastline.

What began as a simple home aquarium soon grew into a lifelong pursuit of marine knowledge, coral cultivation, and sustainable aquatic practices.

Today, his vision continues to guide Langoy Industries in promoting responsible marine trade and inspiring appreciation for the underwater world.
